How the Invitation Process Works:

  1. Fill in basic agent information - Enter the agent's name, email, and contact details
  2. Click "Send Invitation" - The system will send an automated email to the agent
  3. Agent receives email - The email contains a secure link to complete registration
  4. Agent creates account - They'll set up their password and verify their email
  5. Agent completes profile - They'll add additional details, photo, and preferences
  6. Agent appears in your team - Once completed, they'll show up in your agents list
